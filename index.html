About Me

As a Computer Systems Engineer and Systems Analyst, I currently serve as the Head of the Technological Development Department at FES Iztacala, a distinguished faculty of the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM).

My department's primary mission centers on the strategic implementation and management of the Faculty's Learning Management Systems (LMS). We are dedicated to continuous monitoring, maintenance, and performance analysis of these critical educational platforms, ensuring optimal delivery of distance education services.

Beyond technical infrastructure, my work encompasses the transformative impact of technology on pedagogical methods and comprehensive training in Information and Communication Technologies (ICT). We actively monitor student and faculty engagement within distance education systems and analyze their integration with social networks, driving innovation in digital education delivery.

With a unique blend of technical expertise and educational insight, stemming from my background in both Computer Systems Engineering and Biology, I bridge the gap between technology and academia, creating innovative solutions that enhance the learning experience for thousands of students.
